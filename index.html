Trumpington CC Sports Hall

The Trumpington Community College's Sports Hall is a massive space used for Physical Education (PE) and other lessons like drama. You would have a lot fun echoing your voice through this void!

Trumpington CC Science Lab

The Trumpington Community College's Science Lab is a room used for school science experiments. There are water taps, gas jets and even a closable sink! If you love experiments, then you should come here and enjoy the fun!

TCC Open Evening

Trumpington Community college's Open Evening Event is an event where several students make speeches and welcome new students and their parents every year. Grade 6 students come to Trumpington Community College to see how great it is and if they want to join in their first year of high school education. Parents are also welcomed to check out this magnificent school and see whether it is perfect for their children/child.

  • New School Chromebooks!

    New school chromebooks are being released in Trumpington Community College! This school provides chromebooks so that students can be more arranged and can work more easily. Chromebooks can be used for online homework, online tests and even work on Microsoft Teams!
